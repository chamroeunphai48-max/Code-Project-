Birth Place of The Buddha

The Lord Buddha was born in 623 BC in the sacred area of Lumbini located in the Terai plains of southern Nepal, testified by the inscription on the pillar erected by the Mauryan Emperor Asoka in 249 BC. according to Buddhist tradition. He was called Siddhartha Gautama in his childhood.

Birth Place of The Buddha

Name: He was born Prince Siddhartha Gautama. Date: Historically, his birth is estimated to be around 563 BCE, though some sources cite 480 BCE. In tradition, it is 623 BCE. Location: He was born in Lumbini, a site located in the Shakya Republic (modern-day Nepal). Family: His father was King Śuddhodana and his mother was Queen Maya Devi. Significance: Lumbini is now a World Heritage Site and a sacred pilgrimage destination for Buddhists.
